# Code of Conduct

## Our standard
Be respectful, specific, and useful.

This project welcomes disagreement, but not noise.

## Expected behavior
- criticize ideas, not people
- use evidence where possible
- keep feedback practical
- label opinions as opinions
- help improve accuracy

## Unacceptable behavior
- harassment or personal attacks
- spammy self-promotion
- bad-faith edits
- deceptive marketing claims
- hostile or abusive language

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove low-quality, abusive, or misleading contributions to protect the usefulness of the project.
